Who I am

I'm a software engineer at Amazon, based in Brazil. I've been writing code for about six years and fell into it completely — I work mostly in JavaScript but have built things in Python, Go, C, Java, Lua, and others along the way. What draws me most is creative programming: the kind of work where the output feels like it has a personality.

Music

My taste is all over the place in the best way. Lately it's been heavy on post-punk and darkwave — Molchat Doma, Crystal Castles, Buerak — but I'll switch to Haruomi Hosono, Mac DeMarco, or Peter Schilling without a second thought. I've been listening to a lot of Russian indie, which eventually made me want to learn the language just to understand what I was hearing. The Music Universe has the full picture, updated every six hours.

Games

I like games with weight to them — Escape from Tarkov, DayZ, Foxhole, Project Zomboid, NEO Scavenger. The kind where a bad decision costs you hours. I also play Dota, Hearts of Iron, V Rising, Hotline Miami, and a long tail of others. Basically: if it punishes you for not paying attention, I'm interested.

Languages & travel

I speak Portuguese and English, get by in French, and I'm currently learning Italian and Russian. Travel-wise I've been through France, Austria, Switzerland, Italy, Spain, Portugal, and across the US — New York, Florida, California. There's a lot of list left.
